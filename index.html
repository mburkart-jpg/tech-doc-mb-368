Introduction

Alchemy Recipes

Here is an example of what to mix for potions:

  • Blue Mountain Flower/ Wheat:Restore Health + Fortify Health (You can add Lavander for "Fortify Conjuration")
  • Red Mountain Flower/Tundra Cotton + Mora Tapinella/Creepy Cluster: Restore Magicka + Fortify Magicka
  • Blisterwort/Glowing Mushroom: Fortify Smithing (You can add Steel-Blue Entoloma for "Fortify Destruction" or Wheat for both "Restore Health" and "Fortify Health"
  • Any 2 of Mudcrab Cithin/Juvenile Mudcrab/Vampire Dust/Spadefish/Charred Skeever Hide: Cure Disease
  • Charred Skeever Hide + Mudcrab: Cure Disease + Resist Poison + Restore Stamina (You can add any "Restore Health" or "Resist Fire" ingredient)
